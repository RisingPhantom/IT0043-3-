Episode 297: The Obstacle: Seven

While Boruto and the others are forced to participate in Ouga's experiments, Team 7 search for Boruto and the other missing people in Konoha. On the other hand, when Boruto hears about there being an underling of Ouga's among them, he is puzzled by the unexpected person as the "underllng". After hearing that, he & the remaining subjects are sent to the next experiment where they are given a random number card that represents the number of points they have. Ouga then states that the objective of the experiment is to get seven points or higher to pass. During the experiment, Boruto finds two subjects who were killed by someone, regardless of the objective of the experiment.
